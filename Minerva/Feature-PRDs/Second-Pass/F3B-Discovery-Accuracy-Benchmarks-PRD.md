# 🧩 Project Name: Discovery Accuracy Benchmarks

**PDLC Phase:** Define
**Authored Date:** April 1, 2026
**Status:** DRAFT

## 🎯 Problem Statement

While F3A provides automated baseline generation capability, customers need comprehensive discovery accuracy benchmarks to measure conversational AI quality, establish auto-calibaring foundations, and ensure generated baselines meet production standards. Without measurable accuracy benchmarks, the organization cannot validate discovery quality or establish the measurement framework needed for future auto-calibration capabilities.

**Impact:** Lack of discovery accuracy benchmarks prevents quality validation, blocks auto-calibration foundation development, and creates risk of deploying inadequate conversational AI capabilities for customers member support.

## 💡 Proposed Solution

Establish comprehensive discovery accuracy benchmark framework that measures baseline quality, validates conversational AI accuracy, and provides the foundation for future auto-calibration capabilities. The solution transforms generated baselines from F3A into measurable quality standards and establishes the metrics infrastructure needed for continuous accuracy improvement.

## ✅ Success Metrics

- **Benchmark Coverage:** Establish discovery accuracy benchmarks covering 100% of benefit categories across all BSC plan types (Baseline: No systematic benchmarks)
- **Quality Measurement:** Achieve >90% accuracy rate for generated baselines against established benchmark criteria (Baseline: Manual validation without systematic benchmarks)
- **Benchmark Foundation:** Create measurement framework supporting future self-healing automation development (Measurement Window: Monthly quality reporting)

## 📦 Scope

What's included in this release?

- Discovery accuracy benchmark framework defining quality standards for generated baselines
- Quality measurement system that evaluates baseline accuracy against established criteria
- Benchmark methodology covering benefit categories, plan types, and conversational scenarios
- Accuracy scoring framework that quantifies discovery quality with consistent metrics
- Performance baseline establishment for future self-healing capability comparison
- Quality trend analysis and reporting infrastructure for ongoing measurement
- Benchmark validation process ensuring measurement framework accuracy and reliability

## 🚫 Out-of-Scope

What's explicitly out of scope?

- Baseline question-answer generation functionality (completed in F3A)
- Human validation workflow implementation (covered in F3C)
- Advanced self-healing automation beyond foundation measurement (future capability)
- Real-time quality monitoring during live conversations (operational monitoring focus)
- Cross-customer benchmark learning and comparison (single-customer BSC focus)
- Custom benchmark editing interfaces (measurement framework focus)
