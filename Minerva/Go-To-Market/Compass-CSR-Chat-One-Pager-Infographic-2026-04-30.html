Product Snapshot

What it is

Compass CSR Chat (Minerva) is a GenAI-powered agent assist that delivers on-demand, compliance-scripted benefit answers using Nexus benefit data and provider contract information to enable fast, accurate member quoting in the contact center.

Core Features
🧭
Real-time Benefit Quoting Natural-language answers drawn from Nexus product data for IFP/SG and beyond
🏷️
Provider Allowed Amounts Integrates Facets/Experience Cube to quote allowed amounts and network-based pricing
🧠
Session & Persistent Memory STM + LTM for multi-turn context and continuity across agent sessions
⚖️
Compliance Script Enforcement Templatized responses and LLM-as-judge checks for policy alignment
🧩
Agent-Assisted UI Chat UI embedded into CSR workflow with quick actions and copy-to-note
📈
Telemetry & Reporting Databricks reports and usage telemetry for QA and continuous improvement
