🎯 What is Compass CSR Chat?

Compass CSR Chat (codenamed Minerva) is a state-of-the-art Generative AI solution that empowers customer service agents with instant, accurate, and compliant answers to complex member questions about plan details, coverage, and cost-sharing. By integrating seamlessly into agent workflows, Compass dramatically reduces manual research time from minutes to seconds, improves response accuracy from mid-80s to >98%, and transforms the member experience across 4 million annual calls.

✨ Core Features
Natural Language Query Agents ask questions in plain English—no complex syntax required.
Comprehensive Coverage All plan types: IFP, SG, Large Group, and Custom plans (2700+ plans).
Formulary Intelligence Precise medication tier, coverage, and alternative recommendations.
Real-time Responses Compliant, templatized answers delivered in <15 seconds.
