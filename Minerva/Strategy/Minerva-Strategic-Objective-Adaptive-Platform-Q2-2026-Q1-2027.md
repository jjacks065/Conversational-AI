## 📝 Executive Summary

Building on Minerva's proven Databricks-based agentic architecture, this strategic evolution acknowledges three critical product realities: **iterative plan data onboarding** requires intelligent quality management, **formulary integration** represents a net new capability requiring distinct data sources and intelligent routing rules, and **conversational AI excellence** demands foundational architectural improvements for sub-second response times and advanced context window management. This intelligent approach shifts from pure acceleration to sustainable quality, positioning Minerva for true conversational benefit intelligence while maintaining enterprise-scale deployment targets.

## 🎯 Strategic Context & Vision

* **Architectural Reality:** While the core Databricks platform enables powerful agentic processing, real-world deployment requires self-healing systems that transcend traditional static ground truth limitations through dynamic baseline intelligence
* **Paradigm Shift:** Traditional AI systems rely on static 'Ground Truths' that break the moment your data changes. Our platform leverages Self-Healing Dynamic Baselines through Discovery (agent analyzes data sets to extract underlying logic), Verification (human-in-the-loop approval and refinement), and Healing (automatic drift identification and baseline updates)
* **The Vision:** Deploy Minerva as a true conversational benefit intelligence platform with sub-second response times, intelligent context management, and self-healing quality systems that maintain accuracy without manual intervention

## 🚀 Key Architectural Evolution Areas

**Self-Healing Data Onboarding Framework:** Iterative plan data integration with quality feedback loops, automated adjustment detection, and progressive accuracy improvements through customer deployment cycles

**Multi-Source Intelligence Architecture:** Formulary data requires new ingestion pipelines, external data source integration, and intelligent query routing that determines optimal information sources based on question type

**Conversational AI Foundation:** Advanced context window management, conversation state persistence, response time optimization (<1 second target), and true multi-turn conversational capabilities

**Enterprise Learning Loop:** Cross-customer pattern recognition, dynamic baseline evolution, and deployment-specific customization frameworks that improve with scale

## ⚠️ Risk & Mitigation Profile

**Enhanced Technical Risks:**

**Iterative Quality Challenges:** Risk of quality regression during iterative improvement cycles

- *Mitigation:* Comprehensive regression testing suites and customer-specific quality gate validation before deployment

**Multi-Source Data Complexity:** Risk of inconsistent responses from different data sources creating contact center agent or member confusion

- *Mitigation:* Advanced response synthesis algorithms with confidence scoring and explicit source attribution

**Performance Optimization Complexity:** Risk of response time improvements compromising accuracy or increasing infrastructure costs

- *Mitigation:* Performance vs. accuracy testing framework with clear trade-off decision criteria and cost monitoring

**New Risk Areas:**

**Formulary Data Dependency:** Risk of external formulary data source outages or accuracy issues affecting member care

- *Mitigation:* Multi-source formulary redundancy, cached data fallback systems, and automated data quality validation

**Conversational State Complexity:** Risk of conversation memory providing inaccurate context for complex benefit scenarios

- *Mitigation:* Conversation state validation algorithms and explicit context confirmation protocols

**Dynamic Learning Drift:** Risk of cross-customer learning introducing biases or inaccuracies specific to other customer environments

- *Mitigation:* Customer-specific learning isolation with controlled cross-pollination and explicit validation gates
