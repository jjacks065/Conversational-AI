## 📝 Executive Summary

Building on Minerva's proven Databricks-based agentic architecture, this strategic evolution acknowledges a critical business reality: **current per-query costs of $0.25-$0.27 threaten long-term profitability and scaling viability**. This cost-optimized strategy maintains all core capabilities—iterative plan data onboarding with intelligent quality management, formulary integration, and conversational AI excellence—while establishing a hard ceiling of **$0.08 per query** through aggressive architectural optimization, intelligent resource management, and operational efficiency improvements. This approach shifts from capability acceleration to sustainable unit economics, positioning Minerva for profitable conversational benefit intelligence at enterprise scale.

## 🎯 Strategic Context & Vision

* **Economic Reality:** Current operational costs of $0.25-$0.27 per query create unsustainable unit economics that will prevent profitable scaling regardless of capability sophistication
* **Cost Optimization Imperative:** Achieve 70% per-query cost reduction through architectural efficiency, intelligent resource allocation, and operational optimization while maintaining quality standards
* **Architectural Reality:** While the core Databricks platform enables powerful agentic processing, real-world profitability requires cost-efficient self-healing systems that optimize computational resources without compromising accuracy
* **Paradigm Shift:** Traditional AI systems optimize for capability without considering unit economics. Our platform leverages Cost-Intelligent Self-Healing Dynamic Baselines through Discovery (resource-efficient agent analysis), Verification (cost-aware human-in-the-loop processes), and Healing (cost-optimized automatic updates)
* **The Vision:** Deploy Minerva as a profitable conversational benefit intelligence platform with sub-second response times, intelligent context management, and cost-optimized self-healing quality systems that maintain accuracy at <$0.08 per query

## 🚀 Key Architectural Evolution Areas

**Cost-Optimized Self-Healing Framework:** Resource-efficient iterative plan data integration with cost-aware quality feedback loops, automated adjustment detection using optimized compute, and progressive accuracy improvements through cost-effective deployment cycles

**Efficient Multi-Source Intelligence:** Formulary data with cost-optimized ingestion pipelines, intelligent caching strategies, and resource-aware query routing that balances information quality with computational cost
